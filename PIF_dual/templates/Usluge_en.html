Services We Provide

PIFPro expert team has as goal to help You develop and place your cosmetic products on the markets of Republic of Serbia and EC. Our services include:

• Preparation and compilation of the Product Information File (PIF) for any category of cosmetic products, including checking and preparing all required documents, preparation of the Cosmetic Product Safety Report, and compilation of all submitted files into PIF;

• Safety assessment of cosmetic product done by PhDs with more than 20 years of first-hand experience in the cosmetic industry, who have additional education coming from the European’s top experts in this field;

• Position and responsibilities of the Responsible Person (RP) as well as consulting;

• Challenge test - Consulting;

• Claims substantiation study - Consulting;

• Irritational potential assessment & Patch testing;

• Consulting regarding any aspect of cosmetic product development - from preformulation studies, through formulation, physicochemical characterization and stability evaluation, evaluation of efficacy and safety, to on site Scale-Up process.

Data & Documents needed for Cosmetic Product Safety Assessment (CPSR)

• Administrative data - cosmetic product trade name, manufacturer data, responsible person data, picture of cosmetic product and declaration;

• Qualitative and quantitative composition of cosmetic product - exhaustive list of ingredients including their trade names, names of ingredients producers/suppliers, INCI names, CAS numbers, functions in the products and concentrations of all listed ingredients;

• Data regarding raw materials - Certificate of Analysis (CoA), Technical Data Sheet/Product Data Sheet (TDS/PDS), Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS);

• Packaging data - specification of the packaging (manufacturer, material, volume); MSDS; Food Grade Statement (if available - if not, additional packaging testing is mandatory); Results of specific tests usually conducted as a part of Stability study (please check "Stability study" below);

• Data in relation to perfume composition (allergens, MSDS);

• Description and/or diagram of production process of cosmetic product;

• GMP/ISO 22716 certificate or signed Statement of compliance of cosmetic product manufacturing process with the principles of GMP;

• Specification of the finished product;

• Stability study of the finished product including study of compatibility with the packaging (cyclic temperature stress test, accelerated aging, normal aging); Specific data with regard to the packaging as a result of Compatibility test, Migration test, Leakage test, visual inspection of the packaging for damage); Experimental data or reasoning for Period After Opening (PAO) and/or minimum durability period;

• Preservatives efficacy test (Challenge test);

• Claim Substantiation Study;

• Irritation potential screening (Patch test) (optional);

• Allergens screening analysis (optional);

• Statement and data regarding undesirable and serious undesirable effects;

• Statement regarding Carcinogenic, Mutagenic, or Toxic for Reproduction substances;

• If applicable: Nanomaterials statement;

• Animal testing statement.
