About PIFPro Team Get to know us

Team working for you has more than 20 years of first-hand experience in formulating cosmetics recognized by quality and cost-efficiency (where needed) on domestic and on the markets of surrounding countries. For every single product your team has also generated quality assurance protocols and parameters and went through scaling-up process.

PIFPro team has two experienced PhDs to guide you seamlessly through the process of placing products on the market in compliance with the Regulation 1223/2009 & National Regulation. We published a number of scientific papers in international journals, and amongst others, papers concerning rapid evaluation of physical stability for the method we devised.

To provide you the best service possible, besides being in the know, we continuously invest in further developing and specializing our team, including trainings conducted by leading world’s experts in the field.
